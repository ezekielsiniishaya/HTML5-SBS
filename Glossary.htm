Glossary

Gardening Terms

Here are some useful gardening terms

Acid Soil
Soil that is lower than 7.0 ph. Acidity is measured in by the amount of calcium in the soil. The opposite of acidic soil soil is alkaline soil.
Deciduous
A tree or plant that loses its leaves at the end of the growing season, such as a maple tree.
Fungus
A primitive, non-vascular, non-photosynthetic form of plant life. Examples include mildews, moles, and mushrooms.
Nematode
A microscopic roundworm that lives in the soil. There are both harmful and beneficial nematodes. Harmful ones take their toll on the roots of the plant.
