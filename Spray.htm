Spraying Techniques for Fruit Trees

For best results, you must apply some pesticides to your fruit trees. The goal, of course, is to get the best fruit with the least amount of chemicals. This guide provides some tips and tchniques for achieving this goal.

Equipment

Unfortunately, most people lack the proper equipment. Most hobbyists have either a handheld tank sprayer that required pumping, or a sprayer that fits on the end of a hose. The pump sprayer applies chemicals accurately, and enables the proper mixing to be done, but the range is limited and upper branches may be missed. Hose-end sprayers can reaach high up, but it is difficult to properly guage the mix due to fluctuations in water pressure. The mix of water to chemical is an essential factor, so we do not reccomend hose-end sprayer. Either use a tank sprayer (and stand on a ladder if needed), rent equipment, or hire professonals to do the spraying.
